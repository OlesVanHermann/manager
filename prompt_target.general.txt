## OVERVIEW

NAV1 "General" regroupe les fonctions transversales du compte OVHcloud:
- Dashboard global
- Gestion du compte utilisateur
- Facturation et paiements
- Support client
- API et tokens
- Empreinte carbone

## SIDECAR RULES

- NAV2=General: NO SIDECAR (dashboard full-width)
- NAV2=Mon compte: NO SIDECAR
- NAV2=Facturation: NO SIDECAR
- NAV2=Support: SIDECAR with ticket list (when NAV3=General)
- NAV2=API: SIDECAR with categories (NAV3=General) or tokens (NAV3=Token)
- NAV2=Carbon: SIDECAR with service filters

## NAV3 DISPLAY RULE

NAV3 is ALWAYS displayed even if only one tab (General), for consistency.
Exception: NAV2=General (dashboard) has no NAV3.
