## 4. APIS & DONNEES

### Endpoints
```
GET /telephony/{billingAccount}/historyTollfreeConsumption
    Response: [{ date, amount }]

GET /telephony/{billingAccount}/historyTollfreeConsumption/{date}/document
    Response: CSV blob
```
