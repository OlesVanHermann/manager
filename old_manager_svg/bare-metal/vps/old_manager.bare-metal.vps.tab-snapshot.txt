## 10. APIS & ENDPOINTS
- GET /vps/{serviceName}/snapshot
  - Liste des snapshots
- POST /vps/{serviceName}/createSnapshot
  - Créer un snapshot
- POST /vps/{serviceName}/snapshot/{snapshotId}/restore
  - Restaurer
- DELETE /vps/{serviceName}/snapshot/{snapshotId}
  - Supprimer
