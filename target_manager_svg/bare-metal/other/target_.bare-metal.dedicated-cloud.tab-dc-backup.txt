## 7. APIS & ENDPOINTS
- GET /dedicatedCloud/{serviceName}/datacenter/{datacenterId}/backup - État backup
- PUT /dedicatedCloud/{serviceName}/datacenter/{datacenterId}/backup - Modifier backup
- POST /dedicatedCloud/{serviceName}/datacenter/{datacenterId}/backup/enable - Activer
- POST /dedicatedCloud/{serviceName}/datacenter/{datacenterId}/backup/disable - Désactiver
